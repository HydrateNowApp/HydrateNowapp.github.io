Quick fixes

  • Ensure you’re on the latest app version from the App Store.
  • For Health permissions, open Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices and verify HydrateNow access.
  • To restore purchases: in the app, open Settings > Restore Purchases.

Request account or data deletion

Send an email to the address above with the subject “Data Deletion Request”. We will verify your request and delete app-stored personal data not retained for legal or security purposes within 30 days. Health data stored in Apple Health is managed by iOS and not deleted by HydrateNow.
